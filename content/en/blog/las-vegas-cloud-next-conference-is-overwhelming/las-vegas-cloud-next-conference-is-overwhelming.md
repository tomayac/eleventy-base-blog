Las Vegas recently played host to Cloud Next, and wow, what an experience! Honestly, it was pretty intense. The usual allure of the cityscape felt… a bit much. All those bright lights and the constant haze of smoke really started to grate on me. And with gambling everywhere you look, it just amplified the city’s already buzzing, frenetic energy. It’s definitely a sensory overload, that’s for sure!

## The experience

Las Vegas possesses a certain chaotic charm, but during this event, the city’s intensity felt overwhelmingly negative. The sheer volume of people, the constant noise, and the relentless stimulation created a distinctly unpleasant atmosphere. It was a jarring contrast to the conference’s focus on innovation and technology.
