Berlin totally knocked my socks off! Seriously, it was such a cool trip. One of the highlights for me was checking out the Reichstag Building. It really gave me a feel for all the history and politics packed into that place. And you know what else I loved? Those adorable little pedestrian crossing guys, the "Ampelmännchen"! They've got these super distinctive little figures, and seeing them everywhere just added this really sweet touch to the whole adventure. All these little things really came together to give me a fantastic overall picture of Germany's capital city.
