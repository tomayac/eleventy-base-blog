Hey, thinking about starting a website with Eleventy? That's a super smart move! It's an awesome tool that people love because it's so fast and has tons of flexible features. You can basically mold it into whatever you want, letting you bring your creative ideas to life. But, and this is a big one to keep in mind, if you're more into writing killer stories and beautiful prose than diving deep into coding, getting the hang of it might feel a little tough at first.

So, Eleventy's not really into those drag-and-drop, what-you-see-is-what-you-get editors. Nope, you're gonna be diving into actual code files and using your favorite coding tools to whip up new posts or tweak old ones. If you're already a coder, this probably feels totally natural. But if you just wanna share your thoughts without messing with code, this setup can be a real roadblock and a bit intimidating if you're new to all this programming stuff.
